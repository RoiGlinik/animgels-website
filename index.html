Game Elements

All units from the original elements page, grouped for easier scanning.

Core Match Pieces
Animals
Basic elements of the game. Combine 4 or 5 animals to create Animgles of the same color.
Animgels
Unleash powerful attacks. Pink and Green strike in a 3x3 area. Blue and Red clear full lines horizontally or vertically. Yellow cuts through the board in an X-shaped blast.
Blockers And Board Objects
Wood
The basic blocker of the game. Unmoveable. Cleared with adjacent matches. Maximum of 5 layers.
  • Level 2
Stone
Unmoveable. Requires special power hit to be cleared.
  • Level 21
Animal Food
Moveable. Cleared by adjacent hit. Blocks special power.
  • Level 14
Food Bag
Moveable. Cleared by adjacent hit. Creates animal food when destroyed.
  • To be revealed.
Multicolored Candies
Moveable blocker. Cleared with adjacent hit from animals. Maximum of 2 layers.
  • Level 21
Candies
Moveable blocker. Colored candies require adjacent hit from animals of the same color, making them a tougher version of multicolored candies.
  • Level 21
Candy Box
Unmoveable. Adjacent hit or special hits will collect a candy. Currently indestructible and permanent.
  • Level 87
Cloud
Clouds are tile blocker. Cleared by a match at the cloud location. Maximum of 3 layers.
  • Level 8
Sleeping Cat
Sleeping cat is an unmoveable blocker. The cat is triggered after 2 hits and moves to a random location while leaving food on his original location.
  • To be revealed.
Enemies And Hazards
Animvels
The Mischievous Animvils are the Animgles special enemies. They are unmoveable. They are cleared with any hit or a swap with an Animgel. Each turn they transform an animal to a blocker (candy, wood, or animal food). Animvles can have up to 3 lives distinguished by their horns.
  • Level 16
Boosters And Objective Pieces
Rainbow Bomb
Move to activate bombs. Strikes a 5x5 area. Combine with animgels to trigger their powers.
Fireworks
Fireworks are helper elements. Moveable. Adjacent hit triggers a vertical or horizontal strike.
Air Balloon
Moveable. Indestructible. Air balloons are collectible elements: drop them to the lowest available cell in a column and clear any blocker in that cell.
  • Level 15
