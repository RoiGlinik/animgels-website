Privacy Policy

Your privacy,
our promise 🤝

Simple, honest, and player-first — here's how we handle your data.

🔍

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information that can identify you. We collect information about your interactions with our game to help us improve your experience.

⚙️

How We Use Your Information

To provide, maintain, balance and improve our game experience. Your data helps us make Animgels more fun for everyone.

📢

Advertising

There are no forced ads in our app — all ads are served by user request only.


We use AdMob by Google to display advertisements in our game. AdMob may collect information about your device and usage to deliver personalized ads. You have the option to opt out. For more information, visit the AdMob Privacy Policy.

👶

Children

These services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone under the age of 13.


If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child, please contact us. We will delete that information as quickly as possible.

✉️

Contact Us

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If you have any questions please contact us at support@animgels.com.
